G. Flight/Tron Stick

Here’s a Wiki page we can use to document a list of games that play well with the flight stick/Tron joystick. Please note you will have to map the joystick either within MAME or configure the controls within the TeknoParrot settings.

The Flight or Tron stick is an analog stick.  When mapping in MAME you'll set the X and Y coordinates.

Namco System 22 Wheel

  1. Prop Cycle

You’ll want to assign two analog devices. The first and obvious is the flight stick and the other represents pedaling. For the pedals use either the pedals from your racing setup or use the trackball. For trackball setup reverse the analog settings within the menu so that rolling down decreases speed and rolling forward increases speed.

Optional Use - Rotary and Spinner Games

(credit Bill Handschuh) Depending on the flightstick purchased you may have 2 buttons in the rear or on the top of the flightstick. If you have those buttons you can map them to rotate your character in games such as Ikari Warriors and Frontline.

3. Adding a Photo to a Wiki

 

1. Open an Internet browser, go to Google and type “Launch Box Big Box Logo,” then click on Images.

 

2. Right click on any image and select “Save Image as...” (make sure the image is a jpg).

 

3. Save the image to your desktop  as “big box” and then close that browser or window.

 

4. At the Omega Wiki click on the “8 Big Box Favorites” page (or any page).

 

5. Click on the pencil (in the blue circle) to edit the page.

 

6. Click under the title “8. Big Box Favorites.”

 

7. Now, click on the icon of a photo with mountains on the ribbon (if you hover over it – it will say “Insert Assets” (see photo). 

 

8.  The “Assets” window is then displayed (see photo).

 

9. In the upper right of the Assets Window click on “Browse or Drop files here…” (see photo).

10. Navigate to your desktop and click on the Big Box photo you just saved and then click on “Open.”  See photo.

11. Click on “+NEW FOLDER.”  You could upload all your photos and graphics to the root directory but that gets confusing, so we will create a new folder for photos and graphics called “Big Box Favorites” so you can store all your photos for this page in one place (see photo).

 

12. In the “New Folder” dialogue box type in lower case letters only; “bigbox” and then click on “CREATE.”

13. Now, click on the “bigbox” folder you just created.

14. Click on UPLOAD

 

 

15.  The “big box.jpg” file is now listed under “Assets" (see photo above).

16. Click on “INSERT” to insert the photo into the wikki page (see photo above).

 

17. The photo is now inserted into the wiki page.  If you click on the photo you have some options how the photo can appear on the page. Congratulations, you just added a photo to a wiki.

18. Normally, you would click on Save - to save your changes -  but because we don't need this Click on “CLOSE" and exit the page.

2. Adding a Page to the Wikki

 

  1. Let’s say you want to add a page to the wikki called “8. Favorites in Big Box.” That is you want to add a page in the wiki and you want to organize it under Omega and under Omega Operation Manual and you want it to neatly fit in the 8th spot in the Omega Operation Manual.” See photo.

 

2. Click on the “New Page” icon in the upper right of the screen. It looks like a piece of paper with a plus sign (see photo).

3. The “Select New Page Location” dialogue box is opened. 

 

4. Since you want the new page to be under the Omega Virtual Folder, under “Virtual Folders” to the left click on “Omega” and then click on “Omega Operation Manual.” Notice that the folder directory at the bottom of the dialogue box is being built (see photo).

5. Click in the folder directory and erase “new-page” and type “BigBoxFavorites” (DO NOT INCLUDE ANY SPACES AND DO NOT INCLUDE THE QUOTATION MARKS),  then click on “SELECT.” See photo.

 

 

6. The editor page is then shown (see photo).  Click on “Visual Editor – Rich-text WYSIWYG” (see photo).

 

 

7. The “Page Properties” screen should now be shown (see photo).  The “INFO” tab at the top of the page should be displayed.

 

8. At “Title” enter the title of your page.  Here, type:  “8. Big Box Favorites”.  This is what the wiki will alphabetize your page on. Since we want this to appear in the 8th position we put an “8” in the title.  If you didn’t care where it shows you could just type Big Box Favorites and the wiki will sort your pages alphabetically. 

 

9. At “Short Description”  type a brief summary of the page.  Here type: “How to use Favorites in Big Box.”

 

10. At “Path” the path should be: “Omega/OmegaOperationManual/BigBoxFavorites”.  NOTE:  the Path CANNOT CONTAIN ANY SPACES OR ANYTHING OTHER THAN LETTERS. 

 

11. Click in the “Tags” rectangle and type:  “Big Box” and then hit the [ENTER] key.  Notice that “Big Box” is place in a green box as a tag.  Next, type “favorites” and hit the [ENTER] key then type “favorite games” and hit the [ENTER] key.  If you make a mistake after you hit ENTER, just click the “X” to delete the tag you want to get rid of.

 

 12. Click on “OK” in the top right of the screen.

 

13. A blank wikki page has now been created (see photo).  

 

 

14. Type the text in the photo. 

 

15. Click on “Create” to create the page.

 

16. The page has now been created (see photo).

 

17. Click on the pencil icon in the lower right of the screen (in the blue circle).

 

18. Click on “PAGE” in the upper right of the screen.  Notice that that “Page Properties” windows is displayed.  You can edit it if you need to make changes.

 

19. Click on OK to close the “Page Properties” window.

 

20. Click on “CLOSE” to close the “8. Big Box Favorites” page.

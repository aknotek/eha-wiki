5. Deleting a Wikki Page

Note: you must have permission to delete a page. 

 

  1. At the Omega Wikki click on the “8 Big Box Favorites” page.

 

2. Hover over the pencil (in the blue circle) in the lower right of the screen and then click on the red trashcan (if you hover over it – it says “Delete.”

 

3. Then, click on “Delete” to delete the page.

Logging In & Editing a Wiki Page

 

A person visiting the EHA Wiki can view content without logging in.  If a user wants to edit content on the wiki the user needs to create an account and login.  To do this, click on the icon with a person (head and shoulders in a circle) in the upper right of the screen.  At the "Enter your credentials" page click on “Create an account.”  Go through the “Create an account” process. At the end you will receive an email with a link to verify your account. Click on the link.  Your account should now verified.  You may need to login (again, just click on head and shoulders in a circle icon in the upper right of the screen and enter your credentials).  You are now a “Guest.”  As a “Guest” you do not have edit privileges yet.  

Periodically, an Administrator of the wiki will automatically upgrade “Guests” to “Members.”  As a “Member" you can add content to a wiki page, but not delete content.  Below are instructions for adding content.

 

  1. Before you can edit a page in the wiki you need permission.
  2. When you are given permission you then need to login to the wiki.
  3. In the upper right of the screen is an icon with a person (head and shoulders in a circle).  If you hover over this it will say “Login.” Click on this.
  4. Enter your login credentials.
  5. Now, notice that you have new options in the upper right of the screen (see icons).

Note: your icons may look different depending on the level of permissions you have.

 

6. Using the left side of the screen, navigate to the page you want to edit.

7. To edit the page you can either click on the pencil icon (with a blue background) in the lower right or point and click on the icon in the upper right that looks like a piece of paper with a pencil (if you hover over it – it says “Page Actions”) and then click on “Edit.” See photo.

 

8. The page is now loaded and you can edit and make changes to the wikki page.

9. Periodically click on “SAVE” in the upper right of the page to save your changes.

10. When you are done making changes and after you have saved your changes click on “CLOSE.” If  you click on “CLOSE” before saving your changes you will get a dialog box that says “Discard Unsaved Changes?,”  click on “Cancel” and then save your changes and click on “CLOSE.”

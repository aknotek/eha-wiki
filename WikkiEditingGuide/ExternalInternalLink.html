4. Adding Links (External and  Internal) 

 

  1. Open an Internet browser and go to www.Youtube.com.

 

2. Type “Launchbox Big Box”  and then press the [ENTER] key.

 

3. Click on a video to load it.

 

4. Copy the video’s URL at the upper left of the screen.

 

5. Close the Youtube window. 

 

6. At the Omega Wiki click on the “8 Big Box Favorites” page - or any page.

 

7. Click on the pencil (in the blue circle) to edit the page.

 

8.. Click somewhere on the page (where you want the link to go) and type “YouTube link.”

 

11. Click and drag the mouse on “YouTube link” to highlight it.

 

 

12. Click on the icon on the ribbon at the middle top of the page that looks like a paperclip (if you hover over it – it will say “Link (CTRL+k)” see photo). 

 

13. Right click in the dialogue box that opens and select “Paste” to paste the URL.

 

14. Then, click on the green check mark. The link should now be created.

 

15. Click on the link and then on the address to test the link.

 

16. You can also link to another spot in the wikki.

 

17. Still on the “8. Big Box Favorites” (or any page) at the end of the page type “Quick Start Guide.”

 

18. Click and drag the mouse on “Quick Start Guide” to highlight it.

 

19. Click on the icon on the ribbon at the middle top of the page that is just to the left of the “Link (CTRL+k)” – it looks like a paper with a horizontal arrow, when you hover over it - it says “Insert Link to Page.”   See photo.

 

20. Under “Virtual Folders” click on Omega and then Omega Operation Manual and then under “Pages” click on “1. Quick Start Guide.”  

 

21. Then, click on “SELECT.”   

 

22. Click on the link to test it. 

 

23. Click on “CLOSE” in the upper right of the screen since you don't need to save your changes - and then exit the page.
